Ergative marker type

Asya Alekseeva

Last update: 2024-02-26

We classified the variants of the ergative marker into r-type (e.g. balʁu-ra fish.OBL-ERG) and non-r-type (e.g. gagaj-e uncle-ERG, gagaj-Ø uncle-ERG). We elicited four nouns in the singular and in the plural: ‘uncle’ (SG) and ‘uncle’ (PL) (animate, human), ‘fish’ (SG) and ‘fish’ (PL) (animate, non-human), ‘river’ (SG) and ‘rock’ (PL) (inanimate, non-human). The elicited contexts were as follows: ‘(My) uncle helped me to build a house’, ‘(My) uncles helped me to build a house’, ‘The fish (SG) ate the fly’, ‘The fish (PL) ate the flies’, ‘The river demolished the bridge’, ‘The rocks broke the roof’.
