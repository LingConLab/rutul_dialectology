Vowel fronting in APL

Nina Dobrushina

Last update: 2023-09-21

In some dialects, certain verbs show vowel fronting when the S/P argument is an animate (as opposed to inanimate, i.e. humans and animals) plural. In the verbs on the map, the root vowel -u- of the singular and inanimate plural may alternate with -i-, -ü- or -ɨ- in the animate plural. In the same verbs, the vowel following the root may change from -a- or -u- to -e-, -i-, -ɨ- or -ä-. Verbs with a~e fronting of the root vowel are not included.
