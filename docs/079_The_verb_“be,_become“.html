The verb “be, become“

Timur Maisak

Last update: 2023-12-05

An existential verb “be, become“ is one of the most frequent verbs in Rutul, with several suppletive stems and cross-dialectal variation in their phonological form. For the perfective stem, the stimulus sentence was “The apples became red already“, with one of the periphrastic forms based on the perfective converb or participle used in the translation. In the value, only the stem is given, without converb or participle suffixes. The gender is unmarked in this stem (in the”weak” gender agreement series, neuter plural has zero affix).
