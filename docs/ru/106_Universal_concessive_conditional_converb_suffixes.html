Universal concessive conditional converb suffixes

Автор

Nina Dobrushina

Дата публикации

28 июня 2023 г.

Universal concessive conditional converbs are used in subordinate clauses in such sentences as ‘Wherever you go, I’ll go with you’. This converb can be marked by a suffix or by a combination of a suffix and an additive particle. Note that only the use of the additive particle on the converb itself was mapped. In some cases, the additive particle can be placed in a different position in the clause; in this case, its presence is not reflected on the map.
