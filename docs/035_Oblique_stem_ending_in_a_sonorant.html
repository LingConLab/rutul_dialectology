Author

Asya Alekseeva

Published

We collected a set of 15 nouns with oblique stems in sonorant in attributive form. The maps show the sonorant that is present in an oblique stem formant: /r/ (value: R, e. g. git-ɨr-dɨ [cat-OBL-ATTR]), /l/ (value: L, e. g. naʁ-ul-dɨ [teardrop-OBL-ATTR]), /j/ (value: J, e. g. naʁ-uji-d [teardrop-OBL-ATTR]) or NO CONSONANT, if there is none.
